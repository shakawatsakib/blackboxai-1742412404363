About Unity Force Bangladesh

A non-political volunteer organization dedicated to promoting harmony, unity, and providing humanitarian services in Chattogram, Bangladesh.

Our Mission

To create a harmonious and united community in Chattogram through volunteer work and humanitarian services, ensuring every citizen has access to essential support and assistance.

Our Vision

To become the leading volunteer organization in Chattogram, fostering a society where unity prevails and every individual's basic needs are met through collective effort and mutual support.

Current Activities

Blood Donation Camps

Regular blood donation camps organized across Chattogram to ensure blood availability for those in need.

Free Medical Camps

Monthly medical camps providing free health check-ups and treatments to underprivileged communities.

Community Support

Various community support programs including education support, emergency assistance, and social welfare activities.

Plans for Improving Chattogram

Healthcare Accessibility

Establish permanent free medical centers in key areas of Chattogram to provide continuous healthcare support.

Education Support

Launch scholarship programs and free coaching centers for underprivileged students.

Emergency Response

Create a rapid emergency response team for immediate assistance during natural disasters or emergencies.

Community Development

Implement various community development projects focusing on sanitation, clean water, and environmental protection.
